Sewage Treatment Plants (STP)

Compliant, dependable wastewater treatment - designed, installed and maintained with proven German technology.

Overview

Wastewater treatment is where compliance meets engineering

Every development that sits beyond a municipal sewer line - and many that don't - needs its own treatment solution. Getting it wrong invites environmental penalties, odour complaints and plants that quietly stop working a year after handover.

SCALE-UP LTD is one of Rwanda's specialist STP providers. We design, install and commission both packaged and fully customised treatment plants, working with reputable international manufacturers including German technology providers such as LKT. Our delivery record includes high-value installations such as a 138 million RWF STP project.

Crucially, we don't disappear after commissioning: we operate and maintain plants, and we upgrade or rehabilitate existing installations that have fallen behind on performance or compliance.

What's included

  • STP design, installation & commissioningComplete delivery from treatment-process design to a tested, operational plant.
  • Packaged & customised STP systemsStandard packaged plants for typical loads, engineered custom systems for complex sites.
  • STP operation & maintenanceOngoing operation, monitoring and servicing that keeps effluent within standards.
  • Upgrading & rehabilitationRestoring underperforming or non-compliant plants to full working order.

Frequently asked questions

How do we know what size of plant we need?

Plant sizing follows from occupancy and water use, not guesswork. We calculate your daily load with peak factors, then size the plant with sensible headroom - large enough to perform, small enough to afford.

Our existing plant smells and the effluent fails tests. Can it be saved?

Usually, yes. Most failing plants suffer from neglected maintenance or overloading rather than fatal design flaws. We audit the plant, identify the causes and quote a rehabilitation scope - typically far cheaper than replacement.

Who runs the plant after it's commissioned?

Your choice. We train your facilities staff to operate it, or we take on operation and maintenance ourselves under contract, including monitoring and compliance reporting.

Why does German technology matter?

Treatment plants are 20-year assets. Established manufacturers like LKT bring proven process designs, durable components and long-term spare-parts availability - the things that decide whether a plant still works a decade from now.
