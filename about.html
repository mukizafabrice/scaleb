Company Overview

Specialists in the systems buildings depend on

SCALE-UP LTD is a Rwanda-based engineering company specialising in Mechanical, Electrical and Plumbing (MEP) solutions. Our expertise runs across the full life of a building's services — design, installation, supervision and maintenance — including the complex systems most contractors subcontract away: sewage treatment plants and lift installations.

We support developers, contractors, institutions and industries with end-to-end engineering that is compliant, efficient and future-ready. Some projects we execute directly with our own teams; on others, our engineers supervise delivery on the client's behalf. Across both models, we have built a combined project portfolio valued at over USD 300 million in nine years of practice.

  • Full-lifecycle MEP capabilityFrom concept design to decades of maintenance.
  • Specialist STP and lift expertiseBacked by German treatment technology and global lift manufacturers.
  • Two proven delivery modelsDirect execution or independent engineering supervision.

Our Story

Nine years of steady, deliberate growth

2016 — The beginning

SCALE-UP LTD opens its doors in October 2016, providing MEP engineering services to Rwanda's fast-growing construction and infrastructure sector.

Expansion into specialist fields

The company broadens into MEP design, installation, supervision and consultancy — building deep, specialised expertise in sewage treatment plants and lift systems.

International partnerships

To guarantee standards-compliant solutions, SCALE-UP partners with reputable international manufacturers — German STP technology providers such as LKT and global lift manufacturers such as KONE.

High-value delivery

Milestones such as a 138 million RWF sewage treatment plant installation prove the company can deliver complex, high-stakes projects to specification.

A growing portfolio

Through direct execution and engineer-supervised projects, the combined portfolio passes USD 300 million — spanning stadiums, factories, hotels, campuses and utilities.

Today — scaling with purpose

SCALE-UP continues to grow on a clear strategy: technical excellence, sustainable infrastructure and long-term client relationships.
