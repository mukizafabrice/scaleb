Lift & Elevator Systems

Vertical transport that's safe, smooth and certified — installed and supported with global manufacturers such as KONE.

Overview

The one system every occupant judges daily

Nothing shapes the perception of a building faster than its lifts. A smooth, quiet ride says quality; a lift that's out of order says everything else might be too. And beneath perception sits a hard engineering reality: vertical transport is a life-safety system that must be installed and certified without compromise.

SCALE-UP LTD installs and supports lift and elevator systems for residential, commercial and mixed-use buildings across Rwanda. We work with global manufacturers such as KONE, which means proven equipment, proper installation methodology and a genuine spare-parts and support pathway for the life of the system.

Beyond installation, we provide technical supervision for lift packages delivered by others, and ongoing maintenance and performance audits for buildings that want their lifts to stay reliable.

Frequently asked questions

Which lift brands do you work with?

We work with reputable global manufacturers — KONE among them — selected to match your building's traffic, budget and service expectations. We'll recommend options rather than push a single brand.

Can you audit lifts installed by someone else?

Yes. Our performance audits assess safety systems, ride quality, reliability history and maintenance standards, and give you a prioritised action list — useful before buying a building or renewing a maintenance contract.

How often should a lift be serviced?

Monthly to quarterly depending on usage and manufacturer requirements. Skipping service intervals is the single most common cause of premature failures — and voided warranties.

When should lift planning start in a new building?

At design stage. Shaft dimensions, machine-room provisions and power supply must be right in the structure. Getting a lift specialist in early costs nothing; correcting a shaft later costs a great deal.
