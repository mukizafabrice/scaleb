Led by the people who do the work

SCALE-UP LTD is run by its co-founders — hands-on leaders with more than two decades of combined experience across Rwanda's infrastructure sector.

Leadership

The co-founders

VM

Vincent M. Mukimbiri

Managing Director & Co-Founder

Vincent brings over 12 years of experience in business operations management, project management and business leadership across the infrastructure sector. As Managing Director, he steers the company's strategy and growth while keeping its delivery culture disciplined and client-focused.

  • Overall company strategy, growth and governance
  • Business operations and project management oversight
  • Client relationships and commercial leadership
  • Partnerships with international technology providers
MS

Maurice Sanani

Chief Technical Officer & Co-Founder

Maurice has over 12 years of experience in MEP engineering, technical supervision and project execution across complex construction and infrastructure projects in Rwanda — including the last ten years as a Project Manager at Real Contractors. As CTO, he owns the technical standard behind every SCALE-UP project.

  • Technical direction across all service lines
  • MEP design standards and engineering quality
  • Site supervision methodology and project execution
  • Testing, commissioning and compliance verification

The Wider Team

Engineers, supervisors and technicians who show up

Behind the leadership sits a team of MEP engineers, site supervisors and technicians who carry SCALE-UP's standard onto every site. They are the ones checking installations at 7 a.m., witnessing tests, coordinating trades and signing their names to inspection reports.

We train continuously — on new equipment from our manufacturer partners, on design tools like CYPE and Autodesk, and on the codes and standards our certifications depend on. It's how a nine-year-old company handles projects that would stretch firms twice its age.
