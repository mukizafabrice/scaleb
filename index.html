Who We Are

A Rwandan engineering partner built for complex buildings

SCALE-UP LTD is a Kigali-based engineering company specialising in Mechanical, Electrical and Plumbing (MEP) solutions. We take projects from first design sketch to final commissioning — and stay on afterwards to keep every system performing.

Developers, contractors, institutions and industries rely on us for engineering that is compliant, efficient and ready for the future. Whether we execute the works ourselves or our engineers supervise delivery, the standard is the same.

  • End-to-end capabilityDesign, installation, supervision and maintenance under one roof.
  • International technology partnersGerman STP systems from LKT and lifts from global manufacturers such as KONE.
  • A track record that scalesOver USD 300 million in combined project value across nine years.
